I should've mentioned this in the earlier message... Anyhow, for whatever
capitalization standard you prefer -- uppercase or lowercase -- Tidy can be
configured to run either way (through the "uppercase-tags" parameter).

As usual, more info is on HTML Tidy's page
(http://www.w3.org/People/Raggett/tidy/). Just use your browser's find
function (usually Ctrl-F) to search for "uppercase" to see that specific
section of the docs.
