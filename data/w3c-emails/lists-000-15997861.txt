I have updated TidyGUI and TidyCOM to the 4th August 2000 version of tidy.
See http://perso.wanadoo.fr/ablavier/TidyGUI/

TidyCOM now supports tidying from a string with TidyMemToMem.
IDL declaration:
  HRESULT TidyMemToMem([in] BSTR sourceStr,
        [out, retval] BSTR* result);

VB example:
 resStr = TidyObj.TidyMemToMem("whatever...")
