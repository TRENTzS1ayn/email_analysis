I've found a small bug in TIDY. When I use the cols attribute on the TEXTAREA element, TIDY gives the warning "html doctype doesn't match content" and changes document type declartion to Transitional.

I guess this is because you can't use the Frameset element (with the cols attribute) in the Strict DTD. The cols attribute is actually _required_ on the TEXTAREA element.
