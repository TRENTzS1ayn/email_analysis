Yes.  But there is no "name" attribute for div.  You probably meant "id":
  
  <div id="sec1">
    <h1>First Order Heading</h1>
    <p></p>

    <div id="sec1.1">
    <h2>Second Order Heading</h2>
      <p></p>
    </div>

  </div>
