> Someone gave me a HTML file with some correct <input> tags in it. Netscape

> wrong. Then I noticed that the input controls are not embraced by <form>

No, the problem is Netscape versions 4.x and prior.  If you look at the
[x]XHTML DTDs, you'll see that INPUT, SELECT, TEXTAREA, LABEL, and BUTTON
can appear inline and do not have to be in a FORM.  Internet Explorer,
Opera, and Netscape 6 preview get it right.
