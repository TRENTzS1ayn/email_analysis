Hello,
 I've been using tidy for a while now and though I like it quite a lot
there is one thing that really nags me. If I put-up something like

       <textarea name="order_feed" rows="10" cols="80"></textarea>
       
tidy will invariably format that to

       <textarea name="order_feed" rows="10" cols="80">
       </textarea>

which will result in a newline and 7 spaces being the initial
content of the textarea. It's not a big deal but I find myself
weeding through my forms manually after using tidy.
