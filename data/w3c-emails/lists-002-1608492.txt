Well, of COURSE not.  That's a DTD for HTML 4.0 Transition,
and HTML 4.0 uses SGML, not XML.  That's like trying to use a C header
with a Fortran compiler.  Use the DTD for XHTML 1.0, or possibly XHTML 1.1.
Go to http://www.w3c.org/ and follow the HTML link on the left to find out
about XHTML.
