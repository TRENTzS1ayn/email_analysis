Subject: Negotiations of Content type, etc.

The negotiations of content type and the like is a key aspect to proper
operation of the "future" version of internet fax. This topic is tending
more toward "job submission" than what is commonly described as "fax". See

        ftp.mfpa.org/tdf004.txt

and other tdf... files. They describe a multi-pass negotiation protocol and
an attempt to distill the attributes needed from a variety of standards,
including T.30, T.434, TIFF, T.611, MIME, and others.
