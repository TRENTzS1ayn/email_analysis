As discussed at the IETF meeting last week in Washington DC, we now have a
mailing list for the extensions group:

<ietf-http-ext@w3.org>

WG Chair is Josh Cohen, Editor is Scott Lawrence.

To subscribe, send a mail containing the following:

To: ietf-http-ext-request@w3.org
Subject: subscribe

See furthermore

http://www.w3.org/Mail/Request.html

for more information on how to unsubscribe etc.

NOTE: Nobody has been subscribed yet - if you are interested in this
group's work then you must subscribe yourself.

The mailing list is archived at

http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/ietf-http-ext/

The home page for the working group is

http://www.w3.org/Protocols/HTTP/ietf-ext-wg/

(this is still under construction).
