Further testing has taken place; I urge anyone who has not submitted
implementation reports to do so.  For the core specification, we are
down to weak tags, TE, and Trailers at this instant, and may in fact
ptentially be within the rules of IESG as soon as we get a second client
to finish weak entity tag testing.  In any case, the core spec is almost
done.  We still need work for the authentication spec.  My thanks to those
who are sending updates to their reports.

I've set up a number of tests on http://zap.w3.org using Apache.  Yves
Lafon has also been setting up some tests on http://jigsaw.w3.org/HTTP.

See: http://www.w3.org/Protocols/HTTP/Forum/Reports/
