The Workflow Management Coalition (WfMC) is working on an XML message set for
workflow interoperability based on the initial SWAP proposal. A first draft of
this WF-XML specification can be found at the WfMC web page (www.wfmc.org). A
WfMC working group is working on a revision of this proposal; results are
expected in November.
