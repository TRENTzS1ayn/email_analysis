ArribAves

1st Meeting of Birdwatchers of Douro Valley

Bemposta, Mogadouro, Portugal, 25-26 July 2003

http://arribaves2003.marcos-e-marcos.pt

Objectives:

ArribAves is a first Meeting of the Birdwatchers of the Douro Valley for
which the Solar dos Marcos (Marcos Manor House) is locally responsible
through its programme for the active promotion of tourism. This meeting
seeks to bring together people who wish to observe, study, investigate,
safeguard and protect birds from a professional or leisure time point of
view or simply because of their curiosity about the subject. A central theme
will be the bird species that have their natural habitat in the cliff sides
of the Douro River or in the region demarcated by the Parque Natural do
Douro Internacional (PNDI) [Natural Park of the International Douro] and the
Parque de Los Arribes del Duero across the border in Spain.

This event aims to be the first of a series to take place annually in the
village of Bemposta in the absolute heart of the PNDI, which, as a first
step, aim to promote (the so-called) alternative tourism that includes
ecological tourism such as birdwatching, which is much in vogue in foreign
countries such as the United States of America, the United Kingdom or in the
North of Europe, but still little of little importance in our country. On
the other hand, the organisers hope that the event will serve to set the
stage to promote action around the theme of birds, which, in the context of
the natural parks mentioned above, will concern itself about good practices
for their study and protection, which contains a more scientific aspect.
The meeting will run for two days. The first being intended for the
presentation of national or Iberian papers (given the cross-border nature of
the region) followed by discussions and ending with an exhibition of
photography and a convivial dinner.
At present the second day will be devoted to direct contact with the PNDI,
and the realisation of boat trips on the branch of the river Douro between
the Bemposta and Picote dams, walking tours, visits to typical villages and
birdwatching other than in locations previously assigned are currently being
considered.


Format:
ArribAves 2003 will be in the format of a scientific and promotional event
with the oral presentation of papers, round tables and invited speakers.
Completed papers and posters should be submitted as soon as possible. The
best papers will be published in the form of a book and/or a brochure.


Themes:
All areas related to the Study, Protection and Observation of Birds as well
case studies and experiments.

Local Organisation:
Solar dos Marcos
