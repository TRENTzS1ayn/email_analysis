I'm trying to figure out which of these should be used with SKOS concepts.

E.g.

Let's say I have a concept scheme.  In my scheme is defined the following
concept:

<skos:Concept rdf:about="urn:swad-e:example/concept/FastFood">
<skos:prefLabel>Fast food</skos:prefLabel>
<skos:inScheme rdf:resource="urn:swad-e:example/thesaurus"/>
</skos:Concept>

Which of the following two options should we recommend using (?) :

(1)

<rdf:Description rdf:about="http://foo.com/aWebPage.html">
<dc:subject rdf:resource="urn:swad-e:example/concept/FastFood"/>
</rdf:Description>

(2)

<rdf:Description rdf:about="http://foo.com/aWebPage.html">
<foaf:topic rdf:resource="urn:swad-e:example/concept/FastFood"/>
</rdf:Description>
