This message is being sent to "PICS-info@w3.org" a newly created
mailing list for general public dissemination of information about the
PICS (Platform for Internet Content Selection) project.  There is a
companion mailing list, "PICS-ask@w3.org" for the general public to
ask questions about the PICS work.  In addition, PICS maintains a set
of Web pages at http://www.w3.org/PICS/

Please publicize the existence of these lists.  People can subscribe
to PICS-info by sending email to "PICS-info-request@w3.org" with the
word "subscribe" in the Subject: field.  You may remove your
subscription by send email to "PICS-info-request@w3.org" with the word
"unsubscribe" in the Subject: field.

Welcome!
