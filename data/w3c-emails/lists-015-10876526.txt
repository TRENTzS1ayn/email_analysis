> I thought of a service you could propose : a single URL to find the 
> specification of a given HTML markup. I give you 2 examples :
>
> http://w3.org/shortcut/html?q=body will redirect to :
> http://www.w3.org/TR/html4/struct/global.html#edef-BODY
>
> http://w3.org/shortcut/html?q=caption will redirect to :
> http://www.w3.org/TR/html4/struct/tables.html#edef-CAPTION
>
> The benefit of such a service is the capability to automate links 
> creation in documentations, to create bookmarks in our browser.
>
> Of course, there could be other specifications single link, eg:
> http://w3.org/shortcut/css?q=%s
> http://w3.org/shortcut/xslt?q=%s
> ...
>
> The single link is not a replacement, just an imporvement which could 
> be given in the W3C matrix.
