Here you will find easy, painless techniques and ideas to improve 
your Web site quality and make your Web site valid. This document is 
intended for HTML users, developers working on Web applications, and 
Web masters.

Most of the Web sites on the Web are not valid. We may assume that 
this is the case for 99% of the Web pages, but there are no 
statistics to support this. It would be interesting to run a survey 
to prove that this case is indeed true.
