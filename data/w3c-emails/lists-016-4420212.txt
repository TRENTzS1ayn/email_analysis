Congratulations to ourselves !   This announcement has just gone out on
the W3C home page.


The Guidelines, Education & Outreach Task Force (GEO) of the
Internationalization Working Group has published the first public
Working Draft of "Authoring Techniques for XHTML & HTML
Internationalization 1.0"  [1] . Written for Web content authors, the
document will provide techniques for developing internationalized HTML
and XHTML supported by Cascading Style Sheets (CSS). Comments are
welcome.


[1] http://www.w3.org/TR/2003/WD-i18n-html-tech-20031009/
