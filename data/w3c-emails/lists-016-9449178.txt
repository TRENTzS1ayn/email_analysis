The W3C I18N Working Group (Web Services) is pleased to announce that the W3C has just published a working draft of 'Requirements for the Internationalization of Web Services'. This document is near to completion and will ultimately be published as a Note. The Working Group welcomes comments and feedback.

The official version is located here:

http://www.w3.org/TR/2003/WD-ws-i18n-req-20031217/

More information on internationalization of Web services is located here:

http://www.w3.org/International/ws
