Hi,

In F&O sections 8.2.1.1 and 8.2.2.1, the lexical representations for
yearMonthDuration and dayTimeDuration say they allow any "positive
integer" for the values of the components.  This should be "unsigned
integer" since zero is allowed (and signs are not.)  This is consistent
with XML Schema Errata E2-23.
