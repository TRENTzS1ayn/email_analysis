DN-FO-04: 15.1.5 op:concatenate

The name of this operator is very close to the name of the concat()
function. This will lead to frequent programmer errors in using the wrong of
the two functions.

Suggested solution:
Change the name of the operator to more precise and unambiguous, e.g.
seq-concat.
