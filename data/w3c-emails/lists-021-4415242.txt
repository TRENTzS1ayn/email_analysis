I have written two commands 
    http://rhm.cdepot.net/bin/mkrGetData
    http://rhm.cdepot.net/bin/GetData
to get data from the Stanford TAP KB.
These commands run on Linux and Windows.  
They require
    KornShell
    Sun Java 2
    Stanford TAP Client

Here are a couple of simple examples.
$ mkrGetData Person isa ?
  http://tap.stanford.edu/data/Agent
$ mkrGetData Person isc ?
  http://tap.stanford.edu/data/FashionModel
  http://tap.stanford.edu/data/Celebrity
