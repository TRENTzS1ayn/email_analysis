Hello,

The 11 November 2003 Editor's Draft of "Architecture of
the World Wide Web" is now available at:
   http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/2003/webarch-20031111/

This draft includes changes to text on versioning and
extensibility 4.3), on syntax-based interoperability (1.2.3),
on namespaces and versioning (4.6.4). It also takes into
account comments from Tim Bray and Dan Connolly.

Complete list of changes:
  http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/webarch/changes#changes-20030

HTML diff:
  http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/2003/webarch-20031111/diff-20031111.html

The TAG expects to review this draft at its upcoming ftf
meeting in Japan.

This draft does not address the following:

 * Which diagrams to use
 * The running example should use XHTML.
 * The glossary does not yet include copies of term definitions
   and back links.
