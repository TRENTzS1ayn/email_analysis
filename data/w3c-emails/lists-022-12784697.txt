Dear Advisory Committee and Chairs,

This is a summary of the TAG's activity from 16 March 2003 (end
date of the previous summary [1]) to 9 April 2004.

This has been a quiet month for the TAG; the TAG held two
teleconferences during this period. Summaries of those meetings
are linked from the TAG home page [2].

[1] http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-tag/2004Mar/0054
[2] http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/

1) Architecture Document

  The TAG continues to process Last Call comments on the
  Architecture Document. 
    http://www.w3.org/TR/2003/WD-webarch-20031209/

2) Issues

  The TAG did not accept or decide any issues during this period.
  The TAG discussed the following issues: URIEquivalence-15,
  charmodReview-17.

  Details about discussions and related actions are available in
  the meeting minutes linked from the TAG's home page. The TAG's
  issues list is available at: http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/ilist

3) Findings

  The TAG did not discuss or approve any findings during 
  this period.

  The TAG continues to work on a number of other draft
  findings. More information about TAG findings is available at:
    http://www.w3.org/2001/tag/findings

4) Communication

  The TAG sent comments to the I18N Working Group as part
  of their Last Call of the Character Model specification.

  The TAG also discussed architectural considerations around
  Internet top level domains (TLDs) in response to a request 
  from Danny Weitzner. 


For co-Chairs Stuart Williams and Tim Berners-Lee,
