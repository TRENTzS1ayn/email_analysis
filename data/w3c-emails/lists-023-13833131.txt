Eolas is talking with members of the Linux and open-source community 
about the use of its Web browser patent, which is at the heart of its 
high-profile infringement case against Microsoft, the company's founder 
said on Wednesday. "We're in discussions with major players in Linux 
world and working on plan to resolve '906 patent issue with entire Linux 
community," Eolas Founder Michael Doyle told eWEEK.com, referring in 
short hand to the full patent's number. "The solution will be supportive 
of open-source community."
