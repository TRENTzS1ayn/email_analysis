Richard H. McCullough wrote:

> The xmlns URI for rdfs ends with "#",
> but the imports URI for rdfs does not.
> 
> Shouldn't they be the same?

The XML entity "rdfs" and the corresponding namespace are defined with a 
trailing slash, as these are used for fragment identifiers, e.g.:

   http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#Class

An imports URI points to a whole document and not to a fragment.
