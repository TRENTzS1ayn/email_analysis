>The DICE ontology has been represented using OWL.
>It contains over 2500 concepts in the field of Intensive Care 
>Medicine, such as Reason for ICU admission, Anatomy, Etiology, and 
>Abnormality. The aim of DICE is to provide a means for 
>ontology-based registration of reasons for ICU admission, providing 
>sufficient detail for clinical purposes, and facilities for 
>aggregation at varying levels of detail for management and research 
>purposes.
>
>I hope this information is helpful to you.
>Currently, DICE is not publicly available. If this changes in the 
>future, then we will notify you thereof and provide relevant 
>pointers to the project. Contact me if you need any other 
>information.
