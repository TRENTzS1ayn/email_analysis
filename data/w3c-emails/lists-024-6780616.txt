>  >  2) The definition of owl:Thing and owl:Nothing
>  >
>  >  <Class rdf:ID="Thing">
>  >    <rdfs:label>Thing</rdfs:label>

>  >  I suggest the axiom for owl:Thing be simplified as follows:
