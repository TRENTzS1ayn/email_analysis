Per my action item of yesterday, this is a survey to determine people's 
opinions on the usefulness of particular pattern variations in the p2 family.

In particular, please consider addressing the following questions:
         - Is this pattern useful, potentially useful, or useless?
           Should it be kept for further consideration or dropped?
         - Is this pattern important to YOU?
         - Would a different variation of this pattern better meet your needs?
           If so, which one and why?
         - What differentiating characteristics are important in this pattern?

You don't have to answer all of these for every pattern, but please think 
about them and address them if you have something to say about them.

INSTRUCTIONS
Fill in the form below and reply to this list 
(public-ws-desc-meps@w3.org).  For each pattern variation, fill in your 
opinion between the <myOpinion>...</myOpinion> tags.  Each variation is 
identified by the URI reference that points to its explanation.
