Wonderful salads from "Southern Living"


Romaine Salad with Raspberry Dressing

1 head Romaine, torn
2 small red onion, sliced
1 c feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 c toasted pecans, chopped
4 slices bacon, cooked & crumbled

Combine above ingredients.  Serve with

Raspberry Salad Dressing

1 10-oz jar seedless raspberry fruit spread or preserves
1/2 c seasoned rice wine vinegar
1/4 c olive oil

Microwave raspberry spread in a microsafe-bowl at LOW (30%) for 1 minute or 
until melted.  Whisk in vinegar and olive oil till blended.  Let cool.  Serve 
at room temperature.
-------------------------------------------------------------------

Cucumber Salad with Roasted Red Bell Pepper Dressing

Combine gourmet salad greens, diced cucumber and sliced red onions.  Serve 
with

Roasted Red Pepper Dressing

1 7-oz jar roasted red bell peppers, drained
2 large cloves garlic, chopped
1 c nonfat plain yogurt
1 t salt

Pulse ingredients in blender 5-6 times till smooth.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
Red Leaf Lettuce Salad with Sweet & Sour Dressing.

Combine 1 bunch red leaf lettuce, torn; 1 c frozen peas, thawed; 1 c shredded 
mozzarella cheese; 1/2 c toasted slivered almonds, and 6 bacon slices, cooked 
& crumbled.
Toss with Sweet & Sour Dressing just before serving.

Sweet &  Sour Dressing

1/3 c sugar (Splenda)
1-1/2 T vinegar
1 T diced onion
1/4 t salt
1/4 t garlic salt
1/8 t pepper
1/2 c vegetable oil

Pulse first 6 ingredients in blender 2-3 times, till smooth.  With blender 
running, pour oil through food chute in a slow, steady stream; process till 
smooth.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Fruit Salad with Honey-Pecan Dressing

Arrange fresh orange and grapefruit sections, sliced avocado and sliced 
strawberries over Bibb lettuce leaves.  Drizzle with Honey-Pecan Dressing.

Honey-Pecan Dressing

3 T sugar
1 T chopped sweet onion
1/2 t dry mustard
1/4 t salt
1/2 c honey
1/4 c red wine vinegar
1 c vegetable oil
1 c chopped pecans, toasted

Pulse first 6 ingredients in blender 2-3 times till blended.  With blender 
running, pour oil through food chute in slow, steady stream.  Process till 
smooth.  Stir in pecans.
