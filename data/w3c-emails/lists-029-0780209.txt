Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

Ingredients:
  1/2 cup(s) butter
  2 tsp. garlic powder
  4 clove(s) garlic, minced
  4 lb. beef top sirloin steak
  salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. In a small saucepan, melt 
butter over medium-low heat with garlic powder and minced garlic. Set 
aside. Sprinkle both sides of each steak with salt and pepper. Grill 
steaks 4 to 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. When done, 
transfer to warmed plates. Brush tops liberally with garlic butter, 
and allow to rest for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
