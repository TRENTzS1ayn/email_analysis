Welcome to the discussion list for the Working Group on Distributed
Authoring on the World Wide Web, hosted by the World Wide Web Consortium!
The purpose of this list is to facilitate discussion of issues concerning
interoperability of distributed web content authoring tools.

The address for the list is <w3c-dist-auth@w3.org>.  Suubscription and
unsubscription requests are addressed to <w3c-dist-auth-request@w3.org>.
This list is archived at URL:

<http://www.w3.org/pub/WWW/Archives/Public/w3c-dist-auth/>
