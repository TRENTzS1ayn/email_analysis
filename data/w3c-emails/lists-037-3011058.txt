Agenda (Preliminary):

9AM - Introductions
9:15AM Distributed Authoring
  o Overview Presentation of Distributed Authoring Requirements (Whitehead)
  o Discussion of Distributed Authoring Requirements
     - Note items which require revision, and agreement on how to revise
     - Desired outcome: consensus on requirements
  o Discussion of technical approaches to meet requirements
     - Discussion of existing technical approaches
     - Attempt to subdivide technical approaches into smaller problems
     - Assign people to develop technical proposals

12Noon - Lunch break

1PM - Versioning
  o Overview Presentation of WWW Versioning Requirements (Durand)
  o Discussion of WWW Versioning Requirements
    - Discuss scoping (i.e., include or exclude CM capability, & to what degree)
    - Perform final reconcilliation of Durand & Vitali, and Goland drafts
    - Note items which require revision, and agreement on how to revise
    - Desired outcome: consensus on requirements so they can be finalized
  o Discussion of how to implement versioning features
     - HTTP 1.2 / HTTP NG / HTTP PEP extension
     - Working Group sponsorship
  o Discussion of technical approaches to meet requirements
     - Discussion of existing technical approaches
     - Attempt to subdivide technical approaches into smaller problems
     - Assign people to develop technical proposals

5:30PM - Meeting Adjourned.
