Enclosed is the preliminary agenda for the Irvine WEBDAV meeting.  If you
have any recommendations or modifications to the agenda, please send email
to Jim Whitehead <ejw@ics.uci.edu>.


Irvine WEBDAV Meeting

January 27-28, 1997

University of California, Irvine
UCI Student Center

AGENDA (Preliminary)

Monday, January 27 (UCI Student Center, Emerald Bay A)

8:00AM  Continental Breakfast
9:00AM  Introductions
9:15AM  Overview & discussion of Web Collections and Hierarchical
Collections - Y. Goland
11:15AM  Overview & discussion of Link functionality - Y. Goland
12 Noon  Lunch
1:00PM  Continuation of discussion on Link functionality - Y. Goland
2:00PM  Overview & discussion of Copy, CopyHead, Move, MoveHead - A. Faizi
4:00PM  Presentation on Email access to WEBDAV functionality - E. Stefferud
5:00PM Adjourn for the day

Post-Meeting Activities:
5:30PM (at ICS2 building, office #242) Demonstration of Endeavors, a
Web-based process definition and enactment system
5:30PM (at ICS2 building, first floor) - A private network hub will be set
up so that interested parties can play multi-player Descent.  If you have a
Descent-capable machine, please bring your joystick, and a 10BaseT
transciever.

Tuesday, January 28 (UCI Student Center, Monarch Bay A)

8:00AM  Continental Breakfast
9:00AM  Overview & discussion of Versioning - J. Whitehead
12 Noon  Lunch
1:00PM  Review of Charter, Discussion of relationship to IETF and W3C
2:00PM  Overview & discussion of Destroy, Delete, Undelete - D. Jensen
10:30AM  Overview & discussion of Lock, Unlock - S. Carter
5:00PM  Meeting Adjourned
