Subject: W3C RDF Schema Specification Version 1 enters Last Call

On behalf of the RDF Schema Working Group it gives me great
pleasure to announce "Last Call" for any comments giving reason why the RDF
Schema Specification, version 1 [RDFS] should not be moved
forward as a Proposed Recommendation.

With the publication of this draft, the RDF Schema Specification
enters "last call." The last call period will end on November 30, 1998.

Last Call comments should be sent to www-rdf-comments@w3.org
Note that the rdf-comments archive is publically available [RDFHOME].

Sincerely,

David Singer, W3C RDF Schema WG Chair

[RDFS]: http://www.w3.org/TR/1998/WD-rdf-schema-19981030/
[RDFHOME]: http://www.w3.org/RDF/
