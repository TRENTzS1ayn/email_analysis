The WebDAV WG has two sessions at the Washington IETF meeting, a 2 hours 
session, and a 1 hour session. This post gives a preliminary agenda for 
each of these sessions.

Monday, 1530-1730 (2 hours)

- overview of recent major changes to the protocol specification
- discussion of ordered collections
- Mini-BOF: DAV Searching and Locating (DASL), introduction of a proposed 
new WebDAV-related working group
- discussion of open issues in the protocol specification

Tuesday, 1015-1115 (1 hour)

- discussion of access control requirements and protocol

If you have any itesm you would like to place on the agenda, please let me 
know.
