> Subject: DAV Searching and Locating Mini-BOF
> When: During WEBDAV WG Session on Monday, Dec. 8, 1997 (1530-1545) 
> 
> A Mini-BOF meeting will be held during the first 15 minutes of the
> WEBDAV WG Session at the Washington IETF to introduce the proposed
> WEBDAV-related working group: DAV Searching and Locating (DASL). The
> purpose of this proposed working group is to develop an extensible
> property and content based HTTP search mechanism for locating DAV
> resources.
> 
> Items for the brief 15-minute mini-BOF
> - problems DASL is meant to solve
> - DASL's relationship to WEBDAV
> - go over general requirements
>
