----- DAV Searching and Locating BOF on February 10, 1998 -----

There will be a BOF meeting for DAV Searching and Locating (DASL), an
extension to WEBDAV to provide for efficient searching for WEBDAV resources
based on client-defined criteria.

Where: Xerox Palo Alto Research Center
When: February 10, 1998
Directions: http://www.parc.xerox.com/directions.html
Airport: San Jose or San Francisco

Overview of Agenda: Review Requirements, Overview of Protocol Draft

Pre-register with Saveen Reedy (saveenr@microsoft.com) so we know who is
coming.  

Additional Information can be found at the URLs listed below

DASL Homepage
http://www.ics.uci.edu/pub/ietf/dasl

DASL Charter
http://www.ics.uci.edu/pub/ietf/dasl/charter.html

DASL Requirements
ftp://ftp.ietf.org/internet-drafts/draft-reddy-dasl-requirements-01.txt

DASL mailing list archives
http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-webdav-dasl/

DASL mailing list: www-webdav-dasl@w3.org . To join the DASL mailing list,
send email with a body of "subscribe" to www-webdav-dasl-request@w3.org .
