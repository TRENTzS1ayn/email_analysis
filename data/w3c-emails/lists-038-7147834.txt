FYI.

DASL stands for Distributed Authoring Searching and Locating, and is a 
follow-on working group to WebDAV for searching web sites whose objects fit 
into the WebDAV object model.

For more info:

http://www.ics.uci.edu/pub/ietf/dasl/

DASL has been approved for a BOF at the LA IETF from 7:30pm -> 10:00pm on
Wednesday April 1st. Our proposed agenda is:

20 min    review the charter (esp. milestones)
15 min    Go over related work; role of DASL to DAV and other efforts

35 min    Review Requirements; understand basic issues that must be
addressed
20 min    Go over specific DAV Scenarios that must be addressed; which
queries must be possible in a minimum DASL implementation

60 min Go over DASL Protocol
    -  20  Basic transport mechanism, request/response formats
    -  10  Discovery
    -  30  Query Grammar: defining the minimum useful subset; extensibility
features

More information (including registration, hotels, etc) on the LA IETF is 
at:

http://www.ietf.org/meetings/LosAngeles.html
