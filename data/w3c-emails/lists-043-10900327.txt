A meeting of the joint IETF/W3C XMLDSIG working group will be held at
the University of California, Irvine (UCI) on Monday, August 30, 1999
to Tuesday, August 31, 1999. An Agenda will be posted later but the
meeting will start at 8:30AM Monday and end around 3PM Tuesday.

UCI is 10 minutes/4 miles from the Orange County/John Wayne
Airport. It is 1 hour - 1 hour 30 minutes (depending on traffic)/45
miles from LAX (Los Angeles International Airport).

Directions and printable maps of UCI and the surrounding area are available.
