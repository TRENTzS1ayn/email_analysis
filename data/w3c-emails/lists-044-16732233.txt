In example 3.6 (UTF-8 Encoding) you forgot to include the
required version info into the XML declaration. So the input
document should be

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="ISO-8859-1"?>

instead of

<?xml encoding="ISO-8859-1"?>
<doc>&#169;</doc>
