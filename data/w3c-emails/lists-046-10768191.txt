With the publication of "XML-Signature XPath Filter 2.0" as RFC 3653 and the approval of draft-ietf-xmldsig-xc14n-02.txt, which is now in the RFC Editor's Queue for publication, the IETF side of the former joint IETF/W3C working group will be joining the W3C side in shutting down as a working group.
