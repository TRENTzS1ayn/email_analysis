-------------------------------------------------------------------
  For example, the graph defined by the triples
  _:x a b
  c b _:x

  (exists (?y)(and (a ?y b)(b c ?y)(a c ?y)))

  (exists (?y)(and (a ?y b)(b c ?y)(a ?y c)))
