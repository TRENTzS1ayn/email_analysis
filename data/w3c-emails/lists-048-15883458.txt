9: Cycles in subPropertyOf

Propose: Deleting the restriction prohibiting cycles of subPropertyOf
properties.  The meaning of a cycle of subPropertyOf properties is an
assertion that the properties involved in the cycle have the same
members.  A more formal specification of the meaning is given in the
model theory.

Propose:  Approve Test case given in:

  http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-rdfcore-wg/2001Sep/0362.html
