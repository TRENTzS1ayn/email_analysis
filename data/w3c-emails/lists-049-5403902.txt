Subject: Web Ontology Working Drafts Available for Review

The Web Ontology Working Group has released three documents detailing 
the OWL language - an rdf-based language for defining ontological 
documents on the web.  Comments are welcome at 
mailto:public-webont-comments@w3.org
The documents are:

* Feature Synopsis for OWL Lite and OWL. Deborah L. McGuinness and Frank
van Harmelen. W3C Working Draft 29 Jul 2002. Latest version is available
at http://www.w3.org/TR/owl-features/.

* OWL Web Ontology Language 1.0 Reference. Mike Dean, Dan Connolly,
Frank van Harmelen, James Hendler, Ian Horrocks, Deborah L. McGuinness,
Peter F. Patel-Schneider, and Lynn Andrea Stein. W3C Working Draft 29
July 2002. Latest version is available at http://www.w3.org/TR/owl-ref/.

* OWL Web Ontology Language 1.0 Abstract Syntax. Peter F.
Patel-Schneider, Ian Horrocks, and Frank van Harmelen.. W3C Working
Draft 29 July 2002. Latest version is available at
http://www.w3.org/TR/owl-absyn/.

you can get them from http://www.w3.org/TR/
or from the WG home page http://www.w3.org/2001/sw/WebOnt/

We look forward to your comments.
