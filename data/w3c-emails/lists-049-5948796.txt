Last week "for fun" I created
   http://www.w3.org/2000/10/rdf-tests/rdfcore/testSchema.rdf
describing the test cases terms.

It seems that since the namespace we use ends in '/', it needs a bit
of server admin (redirect, rewriting) to make GET on the test:
namespace URL return the schema document.

Jos and others on IRC at the time suggested changing the namespace
URI to either not end in / or to end in #.

e.g. from http://www.w3.org/2000/10/rdf-tests/rdfcore/testSchema/
to http://www.w3.org/2000/10/rdf-tests/rdfcore/testSchema
or http://www.w3.org/2000/10/rdf-tests/rdfcore/testSchema#

I guess I slightly prefer the latter.

Jos also made http://www.w3.org/2000/10/rdf-tests/rdfcore/testSchema.n3
from the above.
