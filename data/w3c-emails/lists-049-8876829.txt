I should note: the grammar does not need changing.  This in the same
way there is no special recognition in the RDF/XML for many names in
the RDF namespace such as rdf:Seq, rdf:subject, rdf:nil, etc to pick
a few.  See
  http://www.w3.org/TR/2002/WD-rdf-syntax-grammar-20021108/#section-Namespace

Only names used for the RDF/XML syntax need recognising in the grammar:
  http://www.w3.org/TR/2002/WD-rdf-syntax-grammar-20021108/#syntaxTerms
