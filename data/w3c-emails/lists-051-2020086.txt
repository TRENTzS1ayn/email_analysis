Suggested rewordings:

>rdf:List
>label: List
>comment: The class of RDF lists.
>
>rdf:first
>label: first
>comment: The first item in an RDF list. Also often called the head.
>
>rdf:rest
>label: rest
>comment: The rest of an RDF list after the first item. Also often 
>called the tail.
>
>rdf:nil
>label: nil
>comment: The empty list, with no items in it. If the rest of a list 
>is nil then the list has no more items in it.
>
>rdfs:Datatype
>label: Datatype
>comment: The class of datatypes.
