14: Last Call Entrance Criteria

According to the W3C process document:

  http://www.w3.org/Consortium/Process-20010719/

[[Entrance criteria. Before advancing a technical report to Last Call Working Draft, the Working Group must:

   1. fulfill the relevant requirements of the Working Group charter and those of any accompanying requirements documents, or document which relevant requirements they have not fulfilled;
   2. formally address all issues raised by Working Group participants, other Working Groups, the Membership, and the public about the Working Draft.
]]

The WG charter may be found at:

  http://www.w3.org/2001/sw/RDFCoreWGCharter

Explanation of what last call means.

Do we meet the criteria?
