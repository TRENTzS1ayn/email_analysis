>either will work in practice over the Net?  In the case of (a) it will suffice
>that the server doesn't grok the pointer but anyway sends me the entire 
>instance - that appears to work in a quick random test with HTML.  In the case

Right, and this is the core of my disagreement with using queries.
We either standardise something, or nothing will be gauranteed to
work, in which case, it's probably better to stay silent for the
moment.

for detecting server capabilities like the above.
