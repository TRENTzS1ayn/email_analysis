The problem with URL's is really 3 problems rolled into one:

  1) Naming
  2) Addressing
  3) Queries

If the ERB emphasizes that URL's are to be used for (2), then this
will be fine. Once we get (1) and (3) mixed in, URL's become an
unholy mess.
