Hello all,

A norwegian (nynorsk) translation of the Web Content Accessibility
Guidelines 1.0 are now published at:
www.deltasenteret.no/internett/WAI/WCAG/wai-pageauth.html

This is not a new translation, but it is an earlier translation that has
gone through some quality assurance and are now in compliance with norwegian
lingual norms. The guidelines at the above location are beeing maintained by
the Norwegian Directorate for Health and Social Welfare, which is working
towards incorporation of the guidelines in norwegian, public sector
websites.
