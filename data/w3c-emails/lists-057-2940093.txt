The translation of "WAI Accessibility Guidelines: Page Authoring, W3C Working Draft 14-Apr-1998 <http://www.w3.org/TR/1998/WD-WAI-PAGEAUTH-0414.html>" to Spanish can be found at http://acceso.uv.es/accesibilidad/Estudio/PAGEAUTH.htm

It is part of a general report in Spanish about Web Accesibility which is also online at http://acceso.uv.es/accesibilidad/Estudio/

This translation was done 1 year ago in the framework of an Spanish HORIZON project. The translation was carried out by Research Unit ACCESO <http://acceso.uv.es> under my supervision.

Now, as part of SIDAR <http://www.rppapm.es/sidar>, I am taking part in the translation into Spanish of the current version of Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 1.0. This translation is being done by Mr. Carlos Egea <http://www.accesosis.es/~carlosegea/>.
