WAI Authoring Tool Guidelines Working Group Meeting:

This message announces the WAI AU Working Group face to face meeting on
Monday, March 1 and Tuesday, March 2, 1999, hosted by Lotus Development
Corporation, in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA.

Jutta Treviranus and Charles McCathieNevile have prepared a draft agenda
for the meeting, which will be updated with additional topics from AU WG
conference calls leading up to the meeting.

Meeting registration, hotel information, and the draft agenda are at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU/agenda-au-Mar99>. 

Pre-registration for the meeting, which is for Authoring Tool Guidelines
Working Group members, is mandatory and closes on Friday, February 19, 1999. 

Note that the hotel block opens on January 11 and closes earlier, on
February 5, 1999.  

If you have any questions, please contact Jutta Treviranus, the AU WG
Chair, at <jutta.treviranus@utoronto.ca> and Charles McCathieNevile, W3C
Staff Contact for the AU WG, at <charles@w3.org>.

Regards,

Judy
