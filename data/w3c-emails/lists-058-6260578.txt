WAI Interest Group Members:

This message gives additional information on the WAI meetings, to be held
in Toronto, Canada, on May 15 & 16, immediately following the Eighth
International World Wide Web Conference (WWW8).
 
An agenda page with detailed information including meeting venue and hotel
information is available at <http://www.w3.org/WAI/1999/05/agenda.htm>.
Registration for these meetings is required by May 7, 1999.

The planned agenda is:
SATURDAY
WAI Interest Group 9:00 - 10:30
Concurrent sessions 11:00 - 15:00, of:
Evaluation & Repair Groups
Authoring Tool Guidelines Working Group
Protocols & Formats Working Group 15:30 - 17:30

SUNDAY
Authoring Tool Guidelines Working Group 9:00 - 17:30.

Previous mention of these meetings was at
<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-wai-ig/1999JanMar/0392.html>.
