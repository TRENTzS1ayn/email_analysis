For those of you who are attending the WAI meetings on Sat. May 15 &
Sun. May 16, please note that the meetings will be held at the Sidney
Smith Hall building, room #2129, #2130, and #2131. There will be someone at
Robarts Library to give directions, signs leading you to the rooms and here
are written directions and a map attached.

To find the meeting place via the accessible route:
- from Robarts Library at 130 St. George (where the ATRC is located),
travel South on St.
George to Harbord Street,
- go West on Harbord to Huron Street
- travel South on Huron Street ,
- then turn left onto the accessible ramp, into the North Tower of the
Sydney Smith Hall building,
- then take the elevator up to the 2nd floor, and take the hallway to
the South building, where you will find rooms 2129, 2130, and 2131.

Directions have also been indicated in red on the attached visual map.

See you there.
