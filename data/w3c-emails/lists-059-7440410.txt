The next Face to Face meeting of the working group will take place in Bristol
on the 6th and 7 of October (Friday afternoon and Saturday).

The Friday afternoon session will be a joint meeting with the Web Content
Accessibility Guidelines working group. The main focus of this session will
be to look at using the upcoming version 2.0 of WCAG.

The main agenda for Saturday is to work on techniques and test procedures.

The meeting page is available at http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU/meetings/f2f-oct00
and will be linked from the AU home page in a few minutes.
