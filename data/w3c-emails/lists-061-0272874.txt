CUTSD is a multi-million dollar funding body in Australia supporting research
and development in the area of Education, mostly within Australian
universities. It features accessibility information including reference to
WAI, Bobby, Australian policy including notes from the Human Rights and Equal
Opportunities Commission and the Australian Tertiary Institutions policy on
accessibilty (and the Sunrise Accessibility CD).
http://services.canberra.edu.au/CUTSD/announce/disaccess.html

Spectrum, published by IEEE had a very good article on web accessibility.
(thanks to Johan Hjelm for pointing it out)

the HTML Writers' Guild launched their AWARE site recently,
http://aware.hwg.org

The Australian federal government is offering AUD $1.5 million in grants to
projects which get disabled people online.
