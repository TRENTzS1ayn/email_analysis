2) Quick Tips bookmark format
- this is a reformating of WAI Quick Tips into a bookmark format. Could be
used as give-aways, or could be promoted as bookjacket fold-out pieces for
Web design books. Text is same as
http://www.w3.org/WAI/References/QuickTips. Format is WAI Domain Logo at
top; W3C logo (long one) across bottom; tips running horizontally across
vertical bookmark; "Quick Tips to Make Accessible Web Sites" italicized
running up left side, inverted white text in black band; "For Complete
Guidelines & Checklist: www.w3.org/WAI" italicized running up right side,
same inverted band of text.
- gif of this format is available at: http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/bookmark0.gif
