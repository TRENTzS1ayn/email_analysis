ASSETS 2000 Conference

Call for Papers, Panels, and Informational Sessions

We invite submissions of technical papers, panel proposals, and informational
sessions (due 5:00 PM EST -  Friday, June 3, 2000) for ASSETS 2000, to be held
in Washington, DC, November 13 - 15, 2000. We seek work in any area whose aim
is
to use computer technology to assist people with disabilities or special
needs.
The ASSETS 2000 is a conference about computer-based systems to help people
with
disabilities.  It is sponsored by ACM SigCAPH (http://www.acm.org/sigcaph/).
For details please see http://www.acm.org/sigs/conferences/assets00/call.html.
