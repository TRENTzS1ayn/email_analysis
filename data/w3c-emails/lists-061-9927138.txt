If you are planning on attending the CSUN "Technology and Persons with
Disabilities" Conference 2000, please note WAI is planning several meetings
around CSUN. 

Please stay tuned for details within the next few days in case you would
like to plan your travel accordingly. 

[TENTATIVE!] Monday March 20, 8:30 a.m.? - 5:30 p.m.? 
Web Content Accessibility Guidelines Working Group (WCAG WG)
Please note the new call for participation and renewed charter for this
working group. The meeting would be for individuals that are joining this
working group and able to make the commitment required under the group's
new charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL>.

[CONFIRMED] Saturday March 25, 3:00 p.m. - 6:30 p.m. 
WAI Interest Group (WAI IG)
This will be a meeting of the WAI Interest Group, with reports from and
discussion on all the WAI groups, and overall discussion on WAI. Details on
meeting location and agenda will follow. 

[CONFIRMED] Sunday March 26, 9:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m. 
Education and Outreach Working Group (EOWG)
This will be a meeting for members of the EOWG, or people interested in
joining the EOWG and able to make the commitment required under the group's
charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO>.

[TENTATIVE!] Monday March 27, 9:30 a.m.? - 5:30 p.m.?
Authoring Tool Accessibility Guidelines Working Group (AUWG)
This would be a meeting for members of the AUWG, or people interested in
joining the AUWG and able to make the commitment required under the group's
charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU>.


Confirmation (or not) for the unconfirmed meetings above, and details on
times, locations, and hotel availability, will follow within the next
several days.
