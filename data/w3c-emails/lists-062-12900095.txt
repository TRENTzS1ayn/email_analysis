ROUGH DRAFT for discussion in EOWG teleconference right now (1 August)

Preliminary Proposed Agenda, Dublin 3-5 September

OVERVIEW:
1. Wed 3 Sept, pm: Joint meeting -- WAI, EuroAccessibility, and other 
Evaluation Networks invited
2. Thurs 4 Sept, all day: Best Practices Exchange on Evaluation Training
3. Fri 5 Sept, all day: EOWG Face-to-Face Meeting

DETAILS:

1. Wednesday 3 September, WAI, EuroAccessibility, and other Evaluation 
Networks invited
* 13:30 - 17:00 W3C WAI and other groups do short presentations on their
work; discuss coordination

2. Thursday 4 September, Best Practices Evaluation Exchange
* 8:30 Introductions - overview of the best practices evaluation
exchange format, logistics, etc
* 9:00 High-Level Process - presentations on process for Web
accessibility evaluation, discussion of approaches
* 10:30 Break
* 10:45 Guidelines Evaluation Specifics - presentations on evaluating
for specific WCAG1.0 guidelines, discussion of approaches
* 12:00 Lunch
* 13:00 Guidelines Evaluation Specifics, continued
* 15:00 Break
* 15:15 Miscellaneous Topics Related to Web Accessibility Evaluation,
such as usability testing with people with disabilities; discussion
* 16:45 Wrap-up
* 17:00 Meeting close

3. Friday 5 September, EOWG (Face-to-Face) Meeting
* 9:00 Evaluation Resource Suite - discuss lessons learned from previous
day, and any suggestions for revising WAI EO evaluation documents
* 10:30 Break
* 10:45 WAI site redesign, or WAI Gallery
* 12:00 Lunch
* 13:00 Review minor updates to several documents, such as
Implementation Planning Resource Suite, How People with Disabilities Use
the Web
* 15:00 Meeting close
