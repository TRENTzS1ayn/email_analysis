A number of activities since the last meeting:

1 - Brian Hardy presented to the OZeCulture Conference
<http://www.cultureandrecreation.gov.au/ozeculture/> on "partnerships -
cultural organisations and suppliers working together".
2 - Vision Australia Foundation ran a post conference workshop on web and
online accessibility after the OZeCulture conference
3 - Vision Australia Foundation ran a full-day workshop
<http://home.vicnet.net.au/~webacces/> in Canberra in conjunction with the
National Farmers Federation
4 - Andrew Arch addressed the gathering of around 50 Victorian Government
web managers about upgrading websites from Single-A to Double-A following
the release of the Whole of Victorian Government Web Site Guidelines
<http://www.dpc.vic.gov.au/wovgwebsiteguidelines/> earlier this year which
recommended aiming for Double-A and including applicable Triple-A
checkpoints.
5 - Vision Australia Foundation hosted a meeting of the Melbourne Web
Accessibility Group where we had a debrief by Liddy Nevile on accessibility
activities at WWW2002 and an update from Jason White on WCAG 2.0
developments
6 - Andrew has had a paper accepted for the AusWEB02 conference
<http://ausweb.scu.edu.au/> on the broader usability aspects of the WCAG
1.0.

Thanks,  Andrew
