You can turn off the use of style sheets in Navigator 4.x.  Internet
explorer E 4.0 allows partial disabling of style sheet when you turn off
color and font styles in the accessibility options.  IE does not disable
CSS positioning though at this point, it hopefully will be a feature in IE
5.0.
