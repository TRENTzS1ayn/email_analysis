There is a new open source tool available as a plugin/extension for 
Internet Explorer. The 0.1 version can be found at

http://checkie.orange-soft.com - along with source code.

Essentially it integrates a number of teting services into IE as an aid to authors. There are a number of things being developed for version 0.2 - including internationalisation for people who can't speak spanish <grin/>.
