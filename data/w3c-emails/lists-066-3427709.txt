Greetings to our dedicated and (I hope) well rested working group.  This
note is to inform you that we will soon be starting regularly scheduled
working group conference calls to work on open issues on the WCA Guidelines
and Techniques (and introduce some of the other post-Recommendation tasks
that are mentioned in our charter [
http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/new-charter.html ].  Just to refresh your
memories, we are chartered to exist until the end of November, 1999.

The meeting schedule can be summarized as follows: we will meet for one
hour on the second and fourth Thursdays of each month starting June 10,
1999.  On the second Thursday, our call will begin at 4:00 p.m. Eastern
Daylight Time.  On the fourth Thursday, our call will begin at 8:00 a.m.
Eastern Daylight Time. 

All calls will use the "Longfellow" bridge: +1 617 252 1038.

The detailed schedule follows:

Thursday, June 10, 1999 - 4:00 to 5:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time (EDT)
Thursday, June 24, 1999 - 8:00 to 9:00 AM EDT
Thursday, July 8, 1999 - 4:00 to 5:00 PM EDT
Thursday, July 22, 1999 - 8:00 to 9:00 AM EDT
Thursday, August 12, 1999 - 4:00 to 5:00 PM EDT
Thursday, August 26, 1999 - 8:00 to 9:00 AM EDT

Reminders for each meeting will be sent out a few days prior to the meeting
date.

Prior to each meeting, please check the:
Guidelines open issues list [ http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/wai-gl-issues.html
], and the
Techniques open issues list [
http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/wai-gl-tech-issues.html ]
