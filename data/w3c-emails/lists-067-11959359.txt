23 May 2000
This version:
Editor:
Sujatha N. Marsden, eTranslate

Abstract
This specification provides guidelines to content developers in developing
content with internationalization in mind.  These guidelines explain
some general issues to take into consideration while working on 
internationalizing
anything on the web.  This would help in easily moving the content
from one locale into another without much hassle.
