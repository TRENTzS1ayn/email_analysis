This message confirms the schedule of WAI meetings around CSUN 2000,
following up on my earlier message 
<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-wai-ig/2000JanMar/0223.html>.

Please note that there has been one change from the tentative scheudule --
the date/time of the Authoring Tool Accessibility Guidelines Working Group
(AUWG) meeting, which will be on Sunday morning, March 26, instead of on
Monday, March 27. 

Note: You must pre-register if you plan to attend WAI meetings.
Registration links will be available within the next several days on this
list and on the WAI home page.

Monday March 20, 8:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.
Web Content Accessibility Guidelines Working Group (WCAG WG)
The meeting would be for individuals that are joining this
working group and able to make the commitment required under the group's
new charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL>.

Saturday March 25, 3:00 p.m. - 6:30 p.m.
WAI Interest Group (WAI IG)
A meeting of the WAI Interest Group, with reports from and
discussion on all the WAI groups, and overall discussion on WAI. Details on
meeting location and agenda will follow.

Sunday March 26, 9:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.
Education and Outreach Working Group (EOWG)
A meeting for members of the EOWG, or people interested in
joining the EOWG and able to make the commitment required under the group's
charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO>.

Sunday March 26, 9:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
Authoring Tool Accessibility Guidelines Working Group (AUWG)
A meeting for members of the AUWG, or people interested in
joining the AUWG and able to make the commitment required under the group's
charter. Additional information on this working group is available at
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU>.
