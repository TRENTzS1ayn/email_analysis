Please Join Us. . .

Three days packed with 16 pre-conference tutorials
(half-day and full-day) will be offered on the following subjects:

* Cross-Cultural User-Interface Design
* Cognitive Engineering in Software Design
* Successful Web Design
* Design and Usability Evaluation of Customer Relationship Management in 
Commercial Web Site and E-Business
* User-Interface Design for Work, Home, and the On the Way
* Design of Cross-Disability Accessible Mass Market Electronic Products
* E-Learning: Plan, Design, Delivery and Implementation
* Using Computer Modeling to Evaluate the User Computer Interface
* Tailoring Speech Recognition to Application Needs
* Understanding User Interface Design as Knowledge Management
* Psychophysical Methods for User Interface Design and Evaluation
* Virtual Reality: Design, Implementation, Usability and Applications
* Engineering Universal Access: Unified User Interfaces
* Multimedia and the Web: Standards and Guidelines
* Usability Design of Information and Computer Security
* The Shrinking Web Interface

The three days of pre-conference tutorials will be followed by three days 
of over 800 intensive presentations organized into 14 parallel sessions on 
subjects similar to the ones listed above for the tutorials.  Take 
advantage of the lower registration fees by registering now and submitting 
your proposals for interactive sessions, poster presentations and exhibitions.

To fully experience the fascinating New Orleans, four exciting half-day and 
full-day excursions are offered.  I am looking forward to welcoming you to 
New Orleans.
