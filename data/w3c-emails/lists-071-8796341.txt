These URLs are, of course, unusable because they have not been handled
properly by the author.

1. Surround the URL by < and > with no spaces. Do not blithely paste into
a message. Cf.
<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-wai-ig/2003JulSep/0143.html>.

or

2. Use an URL-shortening service; Cf. <http://notlong.com/links/>.
