Any of these structures can be abused to generate bad pages.

On the other hand, we believe that adding this capability to the
author's pallette improves the envelope of good pages that can
be created.

How to steer the authors into the good zone is the topic of
the GL group.  They are preparing markup guidelines as to how
to apply the language specified in the HTML4 document to create
webs of accessible information.
