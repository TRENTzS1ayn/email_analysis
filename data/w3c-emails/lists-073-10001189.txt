WAI and related presentations or meetings happening there.

 - On Day one, Monday April 14th, there is a tutorial on creating
   Accessible Web Sites, done in part by Jutta Treviranus, chair of
   the WAI AU group.
       http://www2.slac.stanford.edu/bebo/www7/www7tutsched.html

 - Same day, there is a Workshop run by Mike Paciello on 
    Web Accessibility for People with Disabilities 
       http://www7.conf.au/workshop.html

 - On Thursday April 16, as part of the W3C Track at WWW7
       http://www.w3.org/Conferences/WWW7/Announce.html
   Judy Brewer and myself will have a WAI session from 4:50-6:00 

 - Last, there is the W3C WAI IG and WG meeting, Saturday April 18, for
   which registration is available (and required) at
       http://www.w3.org/WAI/1998/04/meeting
