WAI Interest Group Update, September 15, 1998

1. Reminder WAI IG Review of PA Guidelines draft
2. UA WG face-to-face meeting following Closing the Gap
3. Two at-large representatives join WAI CG
4. Reminder of "unsubscribe" procedure for this mailing list

1. WAI IG Review of page author guidelines closes September 15:
Get your comments in (to the Working Group) if you haven't already. Review
request to WAI IG is at
http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-wai-ig/1998JulSep/0437.html and the
document to review is linked from that.

2. Face-to-face meeting of WAI User Agent Guidelines Working Group, October
25 and 26:
WAI will hold a two-day face-to-face meeting of the User Agent Guidelines
WG immediately following the Closing the Gap Conference in Minneapolis.
This is the only WAI group that will be meeting in Minneapolis. The meeting
is open to active members of the WAI User Agent Guidelines Working Group.
Additional information is available at
http://www.w3.org/WAI/1998/10/agenda-ua. This meeting will be key in moving
the UA Guidelines Working Draft towards a Proposed Recommendation.

3. WAI Coordination Group adds additional members:
Two additional members have joined the WAI Coordination Group -- Jason
White, and Steve Tyler. The WAI Coordination Group coordinates technical
work between the various WAI working groups and interest groups.

4. Clarification of "unsubscribe" procedure:
To unsubscribe to this mailing list, which is the WAI Interest Group list,
send mail to w3c-wai-ig-request@w3.org and put "unsubscribe" in the subject
line. Note that the address includes the word "request" -- it is not the
same as the mailing list address itself. 

Regards,

Judy
