Dear Web Accessibility Initiative Interest Group Participant:

This message is to announce the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) meetings
to be held in Brisbane, Australia, on Saturday, April 18, immediately
following the WWW7 Conference.  A preliminary agenda with venue and hotel
information is available at http://www.w3.org/WAI/1998/04/meeting . 

This is a one-day meeting of the WAI Authoring Tools Working Group and the
WAI International Program Office Interest Group.

The registration form for the W3C WAI meetings is available off the
referenced meeting page. Registration is required in order to attend these
meetings, and is due by Tuesday, March 24, 17:00 Eastern Time [US].

If you have questions about attending the event, please contact either Judy
Brewer <jbrewer@w3.org>, Director of the WAI International Program Office, or
Daniel Dardailler <danield@w3.org>, WAI Project Manager.

Regards,

Judy Brewer
