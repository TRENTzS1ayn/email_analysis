WAI IG CALL FOR REVIEW: Authoring Tool Guidelines Working Draft

The Authoring Tool Guidelines Working Group (AUWG) has prepared a Working
Draft which they would like the WAI Interest Group to review before
publishing it as an updated version on the W3C Technical Reports page.
Please review the draft, dated January 12th
<http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU/WD-WAI-AUTOOLS-19990112>. Comments are due by
January 25, 1999. Details follow.

TIMELINE FOR WAI IG REVIEW:
Please send comments by close of business US Eastern Standard Time, Monday,
January 25, 1999, at the latest. Earlier comments are greatly appreciated.
We will do our best to incorporate comments received by this deadline.
            
WHERE TO SEND COMMENTS:
Send comments to the <w3c-wai-au@w3.org> list. The AUWG mailing list is
archived from the AU home page <http://www.w3.org/WAI/AU> if you would like
to view other people's comments.
          
REVIEW QUESTIONS:
Please address the following questions in reviewing this draft. Other
feedback is also welcome.    
1. Is the document understandable and clear?
2. Does the format/structure of the document work well?
3. Is anything missing from the guidelines?
Please note that the following questions have not been considered in detail
by the Working group since the release of the last Working Draft, and
comments are particularly appreciated on these:
4. Are the priorities appropriate as indicated?
5. Can you suggest additional techniques?

THANK YOU:
...in advance for your time reviewing this material. If you have additional
questions, or are interested in joining this Working Group, please contact
Jutta Treviranus <jutta.treviranus@utoronto.edu> Chair of AUWG, or Charles
McCathieNevile <charles@w3.org> Staff Contact, or Judy Brewer
<jbrewer@w3.org> WAI Domain Leader.
