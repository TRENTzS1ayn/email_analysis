2. Question.
In the Federal government, contracts have been written and are being 
written that state the web site must be ADA compliant.  ADA is a buzz word 
that managers and webmasters and policy analysts understand.  Should this 
be amended to say ADA and  Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act or just 
508?  Seems like we have some education to do here in the Federal 
government.  Would very much appreciate guidance on how this should be 
answered.
Karl, is Fed Web's universal accessibility track discussing this?
