> Netscape and Internet Explorer ignore the <br> within a table cell.

Not exactly true. If the last entry in a cell is a <BR>, then that line 
break is ignored by them. A line break followed by any content is 
otherwise recognized. This allows the use of this trailing line break 
to keep the content easier to read with browsers that do not recognize 
tables.
