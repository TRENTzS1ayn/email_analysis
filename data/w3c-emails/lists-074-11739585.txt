Did you ever wonder what html editor would work best with your screen
reader?
Do you have questions as to how to make that image fit in your page, and
you are unable to see?
Do you want to configure your screen reader to work with the most popular
html editors?
Are you a professional or amateur web designer, and want to know how to
design nice looking web pages using screen readers?
Then the html-designers' list is for you.
To subscribe, simply send a blank email to:
htmldesigners-subscribe@onelist.com.
Or, you may contact:
htmowner@jps.net and ask to be subscribed to the list.

instruction of html.
