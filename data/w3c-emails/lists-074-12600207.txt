Location Update: Adobe At CSUN 99
---------------------------------

Location:   Capriccio room --  Marriott Lobby Level 
Date:       March 18, 1999.
Time:       11:00 -- 14:30

The Adobe access team will be at CSUN-99 to meet with access
customers and to discuss current and future Adobe products
relevant to universal information access.

In addition to myself, we will have Liz McQuarrie from
Acrobat Engineering and John Leckrone from Acrobat server
marketing attending the conference  on Thursday, March  18, 1999.

Stop by the Adobe Hospitality Suite in the Marriott between
11:00am and 14:00am on March 18 to chat with us about PDF
access and to hear where we are going with our access
solutions during the coming year.
