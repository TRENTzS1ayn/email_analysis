Dear friends

There is a non official English translation of the
Portuguese web accessibility legislation
at PASIG web site.

Status of this document:

The documented is a translated copy of the
"Resolution of the Council of Ministers concerning
the accessibility of public administration web sites
for citizens with special needs".

This document may contain translation errors.

The original document was approved by the
Portuguese Council of Ministers, on 29th of July, 1999
and awaits publication.

Can be found, in Portuguese language, at:
http://www.mct.pt/novo/legislacao/despachos/cneinter.htm

The English version translation is located at:
http://www.acessibilidade.net/petition/government_resolution.html
