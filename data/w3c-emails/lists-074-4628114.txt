WAI Interest Group Members:

This message announces WAI meetings, to be held at W3C/INRIA site in
Sophia-Antipolis, France, on July 19 & 20, 1999.

The meetings will include the WAI Interest Group; Education & Outreach
Working Group; and Protocols & Formats Working Group.
 
An agenda page with detailed information including meeting venue and hotel
information is available at <http://www.w3.org/WAI/1999/07/agenda.htm>.
Registration for these meetings is required by July 7, 1999.

The preliminary agenda is: 
 
MONDAY        
8:30Continental Breakfast
9:00Interest Group, including reports on all WAI groups
11:00Education & Outreach Working Group
17:30End for day

TUESDAY
8:30 Continental Breakfast
9:00Education & Outreach Working Group, continued
11:00 Protocols & Formats Working Group
17:30End

For additional detail, see agenda page listed above. If you have questions
about the meeting, please contact wai@w3.org.
