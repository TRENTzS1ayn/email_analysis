Please see attached announcement from Lt. Governor Wicker of the State of
North Carolina announcing the appointment of a special task force to ensure
that both employees and citizens with disabilities have access to electronic
and information technology.  This task force is expected to make
recommendations on the removal of technology barriers in the workplace as
well as to ensure that the State web site embrace accessible web design.
