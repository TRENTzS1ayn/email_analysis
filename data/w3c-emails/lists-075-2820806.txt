>The HTML Writers Guild is pleased to announce a live webcast on
>Friday, 5 November, featuring an accessibility presentation at the
>Stanford Linear Accelerator Center's InterLab 99 conference.
>
>Panelists for this special presentation are Kynn Bartlett,
>director of the Guild's Accessible Web Authoring Resources and
>Education (AWARE) Center; Cynthia Waddell, ADA Coordinator
>for the City of San Jose; and Kim Stephens, webmaster of IBM's
>Special Needs Systems.
>

>Accessibility Standard" and is scheduled for 9:00 a.m. PST (12:00
>noon EST) on Friday morning.  The live webcast may be viewed via
>streaming media at:
>
>     http://www-project.slac.stanford.edu/interlab99/
