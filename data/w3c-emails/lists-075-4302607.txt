WAI Interest Group,

This is a reminder that the Authoring Tool Accessibility Guidelines
<http://www.w3.org/TR/1999/PR-WAI-AUTOOLS-19991026/> are currently in W3C
Proposed Recommendation status
<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/w3c-wai-ig/1999OctDec/0312.html>.

Comments on the Proposed Recommendation are due by next Tuesday, November 23. 

We encourage any WAI Interest Group members of W3C organizations to review
the guidelines during this period and to comment through your W3C Advisory
Committee representative as described by W3C process, if your organization
has not already done so.
