WEBLANG on LISTSERV@LIST1.TCNET.EU.ORG

The list really isn't new, just re-incarnated (get ahold of the owner if
you wan the full story)...

This list is for any web languages.  This includes things such as FastCGI,
PersistentCGI, and the like.  Discussion on Zope, PHP, Java, C, C++,
JavaScript, VBScript, ASP, ActiveX, the whole alphabet soup.  Also
includes discussion on setup and optimization of web servers such as
Apache, IIS, and others.

To subscribe send a message to LISTSERV@LIST1.TCNET.EU.ORG with a body of:

SUBSCRIBE WEBLANG YourFirstName YourLastName

IE: SUBSCRIBE WEBLANG Michael Loftis

There are many more commands available from the ListServ(R) server, send a
HELP and INFO command to find out a little more!
