The International Association of Audio Information Services, IAAIS, is
seeking bids on a small internet web-based database job.


The IAAIS Virtual Committee is currently soliciting bids for the
creation of an On-Line Database for the organization.  Proposals are
due on November 29th to IAAIS Administrative Assistant, Lori Miller at
<mailto:lrmiller@ukans.edu>lrmiller<mailto:lrmiller@ukans.edu>@ukans.e
du.

Here are the parameters for the bid:

Purpose: To provide a current and easily accessible membership
database to members of the International Association of Audio
Information Services (IAAIS) and its Board of Directors.

Process:  create a searchable, password protected on-line database to
be accessed through the IAAIS website (iaais.org).

Needs:  Searchable database; compatible with screen readers (minimal
graphics); accessible in limited form (organization name and phone) to
anyone visiting website; password protected for access to full
database; password protected for editing features; ability to print
reports and labels from website; will include at least 20 fields
containing information received in recent membership survey

Other information:  current database is stored in dos based program
called "Mydatabase".  Information from "mydatabase" must be easily
transferred to new on-line database.  A prototype (usable system)
needs to be set up within two months of granting of bid with another
month set aside for input from virtual committee and board of
directors.

Please direct additional questions to Lori Miller
<mailto:lrmiller@ukans.edu>lrmiller<mailto:lrmiller@ukans.edu>@ukans.e
du or Art Hadley <mailto:art@ukans.edu>art@ukans.edu
