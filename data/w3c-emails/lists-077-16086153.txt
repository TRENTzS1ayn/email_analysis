Jonathan. Finally had a look at your oage. I find the mouse-sensitive
drop-down items very irritating and difficult to read ...

The reason: I find them hard to see. Using black background color and white
foreground color and setting ie5.5 to ignore web page colors, the drop down
items seem to overlap. I have no idea what it should look like, but perhaps
you will take the time to check the effect on your system. The inverted
colors are quite common with the partially sighted, because the standard
white background on most pages (or any software, for that matter) is too
much glare.

Just my 2 cents worth.
