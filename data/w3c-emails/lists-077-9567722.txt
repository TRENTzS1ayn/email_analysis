*T WHO: Microsoft Corporation and HiSoftware Inc.

WHAT: Free Seminar on Achieving and Maintaining an Accessible Web site

in compliance with Section 508.

Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act Amendments, which went into effect
on
June 21, 2001, mandates that federal agencies must ensure that all
electronic
and information technology purchased, maintained or developed meets the
accessibility standards established by the U.S. Access Board. Although
federally mandated, state governments and private organizations can
benefit
by adhering to these standards.
