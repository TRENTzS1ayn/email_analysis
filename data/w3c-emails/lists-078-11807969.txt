Dear Randal, Emmanuelle,

The actual text (and what should be referenced, I think) of the law is 
available (in German) at:
<http://www.behindertenbeauftragter.de/download/gleichstellungsgesetz.htm>
(Federal Law on Equal Opportunities for People with Disabilities may be 
an approximate translation)

Paragraph 11 deals with Barrier-free information technologies. Since 
August 1st, there is a decree for the implementation of the law 
(Barrierefreie Informationstechnik Verordnung - BITV) that basically 
translates in its Appendix WCAG 1.0 - level AA as its reference point.

You can download the BITV text (as PDF, go figure!) here:
<http://www.bmi.bund.de/Annex/de_22681/Barrierefreie_Informationstechnik-Verordnung_BITV_als_PDF-Download.pdf>
