There are still some places left on the 'Practical accessible Web design'
course being run by ScotConnect. The course is on 27th and 28th August and
is based in the Capita Centres in Edinburgh city centre.

This course assumes that participants know basic HTML. It is a practical two
day course that covers the most important ideas and topics related to
building accessible Web sites. It is a combination of theory, discussion and
time sitting at the computer applying the lessons learned.

I will be the tutor for the two day course (see
http://www.mcu.org.uk/about/jimbyrne.html)

Get in touch with Pat Byrne at p.byrne@scotconnect.com for further details
or if you are interested in attending the course.
