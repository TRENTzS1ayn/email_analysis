Please visit HiSoftware and Mercury Interactive for a FREE Web Cast
on --Incorporating Web Accessibility in your Test management and Post
deployment Monitoring procedures-Complying with Section 508.--

Date: September 5, 2002
Time: 1:00 p.m. - 2:00 p.m. EDT

Has your organization developed a strategy for ensuring that your Web
content is accessible to people using assistive technologies? Used together,
Mercury Interactives TestDirector and WinRunner, along with HiSoftware
AccVerify enable project teams to manage accessibility through the Web
project life cycle. Mercury Interactives Topaz along with HiSoftwares
AccMonitor Server will enable users to monitor the accessibility status of
their Web sites post-deployment through an automated and scheduled system.

-Verify Web site accessibility in compliance with Section 508 and
W3C-Priority 1-3- accessibility guidelines from the initial design stage
through final testing, and monitor post-deployment.

-Incorporate accessibility testing into your regular testing process.

-Insure test coverage of accessibility requirements using both automated and
manual tests.

-Schedule tests to run periodically as part of your testing process.

-Manage the defect tracking process from assignment to closure. Keep an
audit trail to prove your diligence in addressing accessibility issues.

-Create reports to manage and document your commitment to accessibility.

-Monitor your Web sites for ongoing post deployment compliance with
accessibility requirements, with an automated and
integrated -behind-the-scenes- solution.

These integrations provide organizations with a simple, cost-effective way
to manage accessibility testing within their existing IT infrastructure.


Target audience: Section 508 Coordinators, CIO's, IRM's, Procurement
Officers, Agency Technical Staff, Webmasters, Content Managers, and others
involved in Section 508 compliance.
