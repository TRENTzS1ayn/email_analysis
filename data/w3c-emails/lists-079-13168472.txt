Jaws supports the following in css:

1. hiding elements whose cascading style intends that the element be
hidden,
2. ins+f and ins+numrow5 font and color and alignment information.
3. when attributes and format are enabled, JAWS will tell you when
format and attributes changes take place as you read the page.
