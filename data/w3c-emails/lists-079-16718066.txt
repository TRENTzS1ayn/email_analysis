Dear WAI Interest Group Participants:

Registration is open for several upcoming W3C Web Accessibility Initiative 
meetings, in Dublin 3 - 5 September 2003, hosted by Microsoft and 
University College Dublin. The WAI meetings are immediately following the 
AAATE Conference.

Pre-registration by 27 August 2003 is required for all meetings. Additional 
detail for the meetings is available at the Web addresses below:

         Web Accessibility Networking Meeting, Wednesday, 3 September 2003
         Presentations and discussion on Web accessibility education and 
outreach efforts
         by WAI, WAI-TIES, EuroAccessibility, other European-based networks
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/0903-network.html

         Web Accessibility Evaluation Exchange, Thursday, 4 September 2003
         Presentations and discussion on best practice methods and 
techniques for
         evaluating Web sites for accessibility
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/0904-eval.html

         Education and Outreach Working Group meeting, Friday, 5 September 2003
         Discussion and revision of various EOWG deliverables
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/0905-eowg
