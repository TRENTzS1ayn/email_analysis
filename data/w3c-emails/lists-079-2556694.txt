HiSoftware and Microsoft invite you to a FREE Web Cast on Assuring
Content Quality and Accessibility for your Web site.

Has your organization developed a strategy for ensuring that your Web
content complies with global standards for accessibility and content
quality? Used together, Microsoft Content Creation tools, and
HiSoftware's quality management solutions provide a comprehensive
interface for testing content against global standards for
Accessibility, Privacy, Usability, Searchability and custom quality
factors. 

These solutions, available as desktop and automated server-based
applications, enable developers, testers and project teams to verify Web
site accessibility in compliance with Section 508 and W3C Priority 1-3
accessibility guidelines. These solutions provide organizations with a
simple, cost-effective way to manage content quality and accessibility
testing within their development environments.

The Web casts will also cover .Net Integration of HiSoftware tools and
the benefits for your organization.
