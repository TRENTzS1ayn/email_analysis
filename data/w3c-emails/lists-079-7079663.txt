Dear WAI Interest Group Participants:

Please note that registration is open for several upcoming W3C Web 
Accessibility Initiative meetings:

Education, Outreach, & Training meetings in Palo Alto, California, July 
2003, hosted by Hewlett-Packard with additional support from Wells Fargo:

         Education and Outreach Working Group Meeting 16 July 2003
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/07-eowg-agenda.html

         Best Practices Training Exchange 17 July 2003
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/07-wai-training.html

         Web Accessibility Training and Exchange 18 July 2003
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/EO/2003/0718-training.html

The first day will be a meeting of the Education and Outreach Working 
Group, with a partial focus on training materials. The second day will be 
an exchange of training demonstrations and approaches among experienced Web 
accessibility trainers. The third day will include presentations on "Web 
Accessibility: The Basics and the Latest" and "Evaluating Web Sites for 
Accessibility" as well as an exchange of information among individuals and 
organizations involved in promoting Web accessibility, and a discussion 
about additional training opportunities. Detailed information and 
participation criteria for each meeting is available on the agenda and 
registration pages for each meeting.

In addition, please note that registration is still open briefly for the 
Web Content Accessibility Guidelines Working Meeting, hosted by 
IWA/HWG,  in Venice, Italy:

         Web Content Accessibility Guidelines Working Group Meeting 1 - 2 
July 2003
         http://www.w3.org/WAI/GL/2003/07/f2f-agenda.html
