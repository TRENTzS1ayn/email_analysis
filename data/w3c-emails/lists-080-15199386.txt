In October, I suggested a new CSS media type, 'reader,' which would 
have the effect of making screen-reader functions legal and 
controllable under CSS.

<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-style/2003Oct/0328.html>

The specification has now been published by the W3C for comment.

<http://www.w3.org/TR/2004/WD-css3-reader-20040224/>
<http://webstandards.org/buzz/archive/2004_02.html#a000301>
<http://tantek.com/log/2004/02.html#d25t1813>

Send any comments you may have to the CSS mailing list.

<http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-style/>

Further related news will be revealed in due course.
