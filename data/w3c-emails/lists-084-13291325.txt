W3C User Agent working group response to call for preferences for the all 
working group meeting

>1. Two Day Preference: Mon/Tues or Thurs/Fri. (Feb. 26-27 or March 1-2)

Slight preference for March 1-2

>2. Will your group be flexible about the meeting days:
>      If you choose Mon/Tues; would Thurs/Fri be acceptable?

Yes

>3. The number of people in your group that will attend the f2f meeting

Approximately 8 or 9 people


>4. Will folks in your group attend the Wednesday Plenary?

Yes


>5. What other W3C groups, specifically, would your group like to meet?

5.1. Joint meeting with WCAG and ATAG
- Development of common glossary
- Definition of common terms
- XML accessible strategies

5.2. Joint meeting WAI evaluation and repair, and ATAG
- Techniques for user agents to repair inaccessible materials
- Tools that provide both authoring and rendering capabilities

5.3. Joint meeting with WAI Protocols and Formats, Mobile Computing and 
Voice Browsers
- User interface accessibility issues with Voice Browsers technologies
- User interface accessibility issues with Mobile Computing technologies

5.4 At least a half day for UA to meet by itself for future planning


>6. Are there other groups with significant membership overlap with yours 
>which should NOT be scheduled on the same days?

Slight preference for not meeting at the same time as other WAI groups, 
since there are at least 2 members that participate in other groups
