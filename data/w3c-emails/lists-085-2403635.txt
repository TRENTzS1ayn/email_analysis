CSS1 on the value "blink" for the "text-decoration" property:
"UAs must recognize the keyword 'blink', but are not required to support the
blink effect."
reference: http://www.w3.org/TR/rec-css1-961217.html#text-decoration

CSS2 on the value "blink" for the "text-decoration property:
"Text blinks (alternates between visible and invisible). Conforming user
agents are not required to support this value"
reference 1: http://www.w3.org/TR/CSS2/text.html#lining-striking-props
reference 2: http://www.w3.org/TR/CSS2/conform.html#conformance
