In the Map-Title text [1] the image and the map have the same title
attribute. It is difficult to discern which attribute is being rendered.
Suggest changing name of one or both attributes

[old]
<img src="../images/map-area.gif" width="140" height="40" usemap="#testMap"
border="0" name="testMap" id="testMap" title="Test Map Area">
  <p> </p>
</img>
<map name="testMap" id="testMap" title="Test Map Area">
  <p> </p>
</map>
