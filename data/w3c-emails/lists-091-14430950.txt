Discussions in the joint DIWG and WAIWG meeting identified 5 Candidates
for collaboration:

 

1.      Device Independence Scenario Repository
(http://www.w3.org/2001/di/Group/Scenarios/) 

2.      Additional authoring scenarios 

3.      Terminology and glossary coordination 

4.      Shared development of and cross-group contribution to techniques
repository 

5.      Exploration of what constitutes the range of user interfaces and
the constraints of different users and their interfaces.    (e.g. What
are the different types and combinations of user interface that a site
is likely to see.    For example,  

*        "Keyboard and large display" (standard PC),  

*        Pen on screen and small display" (standard PDA) , 

*         "Keyboard and speech out - only"  (User who is Blind) )
