Hi,

It's possible that your system doesn't provide a support for wide characters
(see if a file wctype.h exist). In this case I suggest you generate a non 
internationalized version of Amaya.
For that execute "../configure --without-i18n" instead of "../configure"
Let me know if it doesn't work.
