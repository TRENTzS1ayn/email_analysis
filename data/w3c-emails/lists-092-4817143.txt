Hi there,

I have finally finished translating the Amaya message files
to italian.  I include here the corresponding tar file,
gzipped, uuencoded.  I don't consider that to be the final
version, of course.  I had many doubts about what was meant
_exactly_ wiht some of the messages.  I have resolved most of these
ambiguities comparing the english and french messages
(even though in some cases I have spotted discrepancies between
the english and french versions).

In it-libdialogue I have left the following message untranslated:

61 Not any view is selected for printing

This is because I am not sure what is meant here (and the french
counterpart, with its double negative, didn't help).
I had a similar problem with the one-liners *-transdialogue.
If somebody tells me what is the intended meaning I will provide
the translation immediately.

I have passed all the files through a spelling-checker.
From the en-* files I have deduced that single quotes (')
should be quoted with a backslash (i.e., \').  However,
this impression didn't survive to the examination of the
fr-* files.  So I don't know.

All in all, I believe the included it-* files are more than
OK for a first attempt.
I would appreciate if you can include these in the official
release.  That could gain more users to Amaya, with the hope
that somebody will step forward for translating the documentation.
Please, do not hesitate to contact me if further work is needed
(but keep into account that I prefer to remain miles away from
Windows and other M$ software).
Please, let me also know if some paperwork is needed in order
to distribute these files with Amaya.
Keep up the great work,

          Roberto
