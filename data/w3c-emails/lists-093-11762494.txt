The MathML specification has changed and Amaya too.
Now tags must be given in lowercase and the right entity is
&InvisibleTimes;

Your example should be now:
<html>
 <body>
  <math>
    <mi>a</mi> &InvisibleTimes; <mo>+</mo> &InvisibleTimes; <mi>b</mi>
  </math>
 </body>
</html>
