The Amaya team is proud to announce the availability of the 2.0a release
of the Amaya editor/browser for both Unix and Windows platforms.

  Binaries are available at:
            http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/BinDist.html

  Source is available at:
            http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/SourceDist.html

  A FAQ is available at http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/FAQ.html

The Amaya release 2.0 is a major release and includes many new features. Some 
of these
features may have fragilized the code.
Some users reported annoying bugs that we didn't detected before due to the 
large variety
of user platforms.

The release 2.0a fixes the most important reported bugs. 

We plan to do a new release in June, so if you detect other bugs, 
please report them to our mailing list www-amaya@w3.org.
