Hello Amaya Users,

Whenever there's a new Amaya release, we upload it to both our HTTP and
FTP servers. This time, however, only the HTTP repository was available
due to technical problems.

conference, the current Amaya distribution (2.0a) is now also available 
on W3C's FTP servers:

ftp://ftp.w3.org/pub/amaya/

Please accept our apologies for this delay,
