Please refer to http://www.w3.org/Amaya for more details.

1. First build the  compilers application:
Amaya/Windows/Compilers/Compilers.mak
2. Execute this application:
   menu File/Load
        Open Amaya/Windows/amaya/amaya.mkf

   menu File/Build

   Quit the Compilers application.

3. Open Amaya makefile: Amaya/Windows/amaya.mak
   Build Amaya appliucation.

Files missing are automatically generated by the Compilers application.
