* Amaya 0.9 Alpha Release  *
*                          * 
****************************

A new alpha binary release of Amaya (0.9),
dating from November 14, is now available to W3C members.

This release is for both Linux and Sun/Solaris
architectures. 

According to the W3C standard procedure, this version will
become available to the public one month from, on December 14.

For more information on the binary distribution of Amaya:

http://www.w3.org/pub/WWW/Amaya/
