I built the jpeg, png, z, and www libraries from the sources even
though I already had these libraries from the FreeBSD CDROM -- I
wanted to be sure I had the versions you developed and tested
against.

The X11R6 libraries were from XFree86 (3.1.2), and Motif 2.0 was the
SWiM package from ACC Corp.

If there is anything else you would like (config.h?, any binaries?),
let me know.
