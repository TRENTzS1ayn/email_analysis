 for those using Amaya on Linux platform, I am wondering if
it is still useful to distribute a.out version of the Amaya
binaries. I guess that less and less Linux installations don't

has proven to be the reason for installation errors in the
past. Moreover, recent Linux distribution (i.e. RedHat 4.0)
don't deliver compilers for the a.out binaries.

subject (please do not encumber the list), I will sumarize the
answers.
