  Thanks to Angel Li (angel.li@rsmas.miami.edu),

Amaya and Thot are now available on Digital Unix (aka OSF/1). The
associated platform name is osf1.

The binaries are available at:
   ftp://opera.inrialpes.fr/pub/binaries/amaya/amaya-osf1-0.95b.tar.gz
   ftp://opera.inrialpes.fr/pub/binaries/thot/thoteditor-osf1-2.0b.tar.gz

They should install using the same procedure as for the other
supported platforms  :

   http://www.w3.org/pub/WWW/Amaya/User/Install.html

To recompile from the sources, get the patch file (only 64 lines, mainly
related to an outdated XPM package).

   ftp://opera.inrialpes.fr/pub/patch/patch.osf1

and the precompiled binaries, Makefiles and config.h

   ftp://opera.inrialpes.fr/pub/sources/amaya/amaya-libs-osf1-0.95b.tar.gz
   ftp://opera.inrialpes.fr/pub/sources/thot/thot-libs-osf1-2.0b.tar.gz

From that point follow the instruction on compiling on a supported
platform :

   http://www.w3.org/pub/WWW/Amaya/User/Compiling.html
   http://opera.inrialpes.fr/thot/doc/Compiling.html

  thanks again to Angel,
