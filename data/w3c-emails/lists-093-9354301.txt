In our previous episode, bill thater said:
>
> how does one configure amaya to work with a SOCKS host?
>

For the amaya-libwww version, you have to recompile libwww with the
SOCKS option.

Go to the opera/w3c-libwww-5.0a directory. Make a subdirectory there with
your architecture name (e.g., solaris2). Go to this directory and type

../configure --with-socks

Then :

cd Library/src
Make

This will create a libwww with SOCKS support. Then, replace the libwww.a
which is furnished with the standard Amaya distribution:

cd ~/opera
cp w3c-libwww-5.0a/architecture/Library/src/libwww.a ~/architecture

Finally, recompile Amaya and it'd be OK.
