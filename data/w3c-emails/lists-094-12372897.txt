Hi Hugh and Daniel,

When we deliver the standard Amaya source tarfile, some libwww files
are available: *.h, configure.
The libwww community doesn't agree with that approach. They prefer to deliver
*.html files and generate *.h from *.html files with a script perl. So files
libwww/Library/src/*.h are not available in the CVS base.

Daniel, could you update the tar script and replace libwww/Library/src/*.h
 by libwww/Library/src/*.html.

For people who are downloading the snapshot they should process differently
than for the standard source distribution. They have first to generate include 
files and the file configure as explained in the section "Getting libwww 
Boostrapped"
of http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/cvs.html
