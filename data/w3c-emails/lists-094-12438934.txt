On Mon, 20 Mar 2000, Vincent Quint wrote:

  > how to insert a row (or a column) at the beginning of a table.
  
  First, put the caret at the beginning of the first cell of the first row.
  To create a row, select Row (tr) from menu Types/Table.
  To create a column, hit Return twice.
