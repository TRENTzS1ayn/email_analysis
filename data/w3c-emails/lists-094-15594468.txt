Not sure it would be a good idea to capitalize element names and
attribute.  Amaya encourages the use of XHTML, which is XML, and
XML is case sensitive.  In XHTML, you must write those names
exactly as they are defined, that is in lowercase.  Refer to
section 4.2 of the XHTML 1.0 recommendation:

   http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/
