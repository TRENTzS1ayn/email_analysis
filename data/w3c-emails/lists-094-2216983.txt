houghtoa@oclc.org said:
> In CSS1 underline, overline, blink and line-through are exclusive

I think this statement is wrong. Section 5.4.3 'text-decoration' says:

  Value: none | [ underline || overline || line-through || blink ]

the double vertical bars mean "non-exclusive or" (section 5.1 says
"A double bar (A || B) means that either A or B or both must occur, in any
order.")

So, in CSS1 underline, overline, blink and line-through are NOT mutually
exclusive.
