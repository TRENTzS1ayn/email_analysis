A new public mailing list www-amaya-developers@w3.org is now available -
 It's the place where Amaya source code, and building problems can be 
discussed.
It's also the proper forum for proposing public contributions to this code.
The archive is available at the following URL:
http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-amaya-developers/
To subscribe send a mail to www-amaya-developers-request@w3.org with 
"subscribe" in the subject.

The mailing list www-amaya@w3.org is still the official forum where we 
announce new Amaya releases. It's also a place for submitting bug reports, for 
proposing enhancements to Amaya, and for discussing installation and 
configuration problems.
