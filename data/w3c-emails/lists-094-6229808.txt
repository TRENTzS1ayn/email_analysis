The Amaya 3.0 release is now available for both Unix and Windows platforms.

  Binaries (Solaris, AIX, Linux, Windows NT/95/98) are available at:
            http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/BinDist.html

  Source is available at:
            http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/SourceDist.html

This version includes the following new features:
- A better support of CSS: margin, border, and padding rules are now supported.
- The support of MathML 2.0 attributes and the improvement of the maths editing.
- The improvement of the table formatter.
- The unicodification of the Amaya code.

For more details please read the page
    http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/New.html

Thanks for those among you who tested the 2.5 pre-release.
