Hello

For the moment, Amaya doesn't understand ISO-8859-15. Concerning ISO Latin 
charsets, Amaya understand ISO-8859-1 up to ISO-8859-9. So, the best way to 
introduce the euro-sign is to use the external entity &euro;. But Amaya 
doesn't use the exact glyph for that symbol.
