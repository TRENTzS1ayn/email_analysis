> The http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/cvs.html and
> http://www.w3.org/Amaya/User/Autoconf.html pages appear to be the same.
> What is the difference?

The first cvs.html page explains how to get Amaya from the CVS repository.
The second Autoconf.html page explains how to compile Amaya on Unix platforms.
There is also a third Win-Compiling.html page which explains how to compile 
Amaya on Windows platforms.

At the end the cvs.html page there is a section "Configuring and Compiling 
Amaya"
that points to Autoconf.html and Win-Compiling.html pages.
