With heading numbering on, using the Home key to got to the beginning of the
line will cause the entire line to be highlighted.

Component: User Interface/Editing

Severity: Normal

Reproducibility: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click Special > Preferences > General.
2. Turn on Section numbering.
3. Load a document with heading levels.
4. Put the insertion point in the line.
5. Press Home.

Actual Results:
Entire Line is highlighted.

Expected Results:
Insertion point is after section number and before first character in text.

This does not occur for bulleted lists or numbered lists.
