> Hi, Styles don't seem to apply to table columns 
> (http://www.w3.org/TR/CSS2/tables.html#q4).
> 
> Test case:
> --
> 
>    <table>
>       <col style="color:black;background-color:green"/>
>       <tr>
>          <td>This should be in black on green.</td>
>       </tr>
>    </table>
> 
> -- 
> Guy

This feature is not supported yet in Amaya.
It's not so easy to support it as Amaya works with the document tree
structure: <td> elements are children of <tr> but have no direct relation
with <col> elements. We should extend the CSS mechanism to support it.

I added this request in the Amaya wish list.
