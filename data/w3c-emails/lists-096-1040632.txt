The attribute required on a frame element is "frameborder" - see
http://www.w3.org/TR/html401/present/frames.html#edef-FRAME
(Strangely enough this wsn't deprecated despite being a presentation
attribute. I suppose the frameset was considered a temporary thing (see the
proposal for Xframes at http://www.w3.org/TR/xframes for a way to address the
current problems with frames...)
