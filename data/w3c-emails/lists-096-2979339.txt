It seems Amaya 8.0 doesn't recognize a list of media descriptors in
XML stylesheet PI.  The following works:

  <?xml-stylesheet type="text/css" href="style.css" media="screen"?>

But the following doesn't work:

  <?xml-stylesheet type="text/css" href="style.css" media="screen, print"?>
