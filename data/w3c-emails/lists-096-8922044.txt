html 4 specification 17.11.1  deals with tabindexing ....  Amaya has
two types of bugs here!! 
1] it does not follow the tab indexing at all
2] readonly elements are supposed to be missed entirely as there is
no point in them obtaining focus.

and additional problem is that amaya allows one to enter data in a read 
only area ....   

sghort demo follows
