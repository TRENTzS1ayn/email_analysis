A follow-up,

We modified the Amaya code so that now we respect the ISO 8601
format, as specified in [1].

We can parse both formats:

   1994-11-05T08:15:30-05:00
   1994-11-05T13:15:30Z

And we output the first format now (with the time zone offset).

The patch is commited to CVS and will be available in the next Amaya
release.

-jose

[1] http://www.w3.org/TR/NOTE-datetime
