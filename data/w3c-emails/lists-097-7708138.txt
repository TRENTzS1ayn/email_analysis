DFKI - Job Openings

The German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI GmbH) is 
seeking for its Language Technology Lab several researchers and software 
engineers, as specified below.

DFKI offers competitive salaries and the opportunity of working in a 
dynamic and successful international team that combines excellent 
research with developing innovative applications.

DFKI is located on the campus of Saarland University. The university's 
research groups and curricula in the fields of Computational Linguistics 
and Computer Science are internationally renowned.

Please send your electronic application (preferably in PDF format) and 
inquiries to lt-jobs@dfki.de, referring to the number of the job 
opening. A meaningful application should include a cover letter, a CV, a 
brief summary of research interests, a statement of interest in the 
position offered, and contact information for three references.

  * 200431 - Three researchers and software engineers in the area of
     semantic web and question answering, starting around 07/04
     (05.05.2004, closing date 15.06.2004)

     http://www.dfki.de/lt/job_details.php?id=200431

  * 200434 - Researcher in the area of cognitive systems, starting
     around 10/04 (05.05.2004, closing date 31.07.2004)

     http://www.dfki.de/lt/job_details.php?id=200434

  * 200438 - Researcher or software engineer in the area of
     multilingual text processing and information management systems,
      starting around 10/04, (05.05.2004, closing date 31.07.2004)

     http://www.dfki.de/lt/job_details.php?id=200438
