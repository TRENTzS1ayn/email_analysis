Regarding you email to the RDF validator list:

 http://lists.w3.org/Archives/Public/www-rdf-validator/2002Mar/0021.html

The "Warning: {W101} Unqualified use of rdf:about has been deprecated."
message is a warning that RDF attributes (e.g. about, type, resource, etc.)
should be qualified with a namespace prefix (e.g. rdf:about).

See the following for more information about this issue and the 
direction of the Working Group that is revising the RDF Model and 
Syntax spec:

 http://www.w3.org/2000/03/rdf-tracking/#rdf-ns-prefix-confusion
