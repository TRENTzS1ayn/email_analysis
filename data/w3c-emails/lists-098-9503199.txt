Semantic Web Science Foundation

The mission of the Semantic Web Science Foundation (SWSF) is to help to bring
the web to its full potential. Its specific focus is to promote and organize
research and education on semantic web issues. It is organized as a
non-profit foundation. To achieve its objectives it runs a number of initiatives:

Organization of the International Semantic Web Conference.
Collecting educational material (in co-operation with Ontoweb).
Maintaining research journals of high qualiatiy (in co-operation with ETAI).
Support the organization of workshops and tutorials around the semantic web.
