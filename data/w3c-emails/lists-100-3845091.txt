The W3C Device Independence Working Group will be holding a public 
workshop on Device Independent Authoring Techniques on 25-26 September 
2002 at St Leon-Rot, Germany, hosted by SAP AG.

The objective of the workshop is to gather input, from practitioners and 
technology providers, of techniques that help authors create Web content 
and applications for delivery across many kinds of presentation device.

For further details please see the Call for Participation:
   http://www.w3.org/2002/07/DIAT/diat-workshop-cfp.html
