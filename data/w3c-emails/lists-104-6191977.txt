The big problem with that is that DOM has a very strong notion that 
each node is always and exclusively tied to exactly one document. 
Furthermore, it cannot be moved from one document to another. Why 
this is so, I'm not sure; but it does make DOM very inconvenient for 
many multi-document applications. I doubt what you propose could 
really fit into the existing DOM structure, which is not so much a 
flaw in your idea as in DOM.
