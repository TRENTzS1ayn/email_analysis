TclDOM - A Tcl Language Binding for the DOM

Version 2.0theta is now available for download from the
TclXML project website on SourceForge:

http://tclxml.sourceforge.net/tcldom.html

TclDOM v2.0 provides DOM Level 1 functionality and partially
implements DOM Level 2.

This release is designated as 'theta' because it contains
only the pure-Tcl implementation of TclDOM.  Two other
subprojects are working towards providing TclDOM implementations
in C: one by Joe English based on tcldompro and one by
myself based on the Gnome libxml2 library.

Please submit bug reports and RFEs on SourceForge -
http://sourceforge.net/projects/tclxml/

Submit praise and expressions of gratitude directly to me ;-)
