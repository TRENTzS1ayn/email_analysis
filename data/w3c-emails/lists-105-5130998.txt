===========================================
        ODRL International Workshop
     Vienna, Austria, 22-23 April 2004
===========================================

The ODRL International Workshop will be held at The Vienna
University of Economics and Business Administration from
Thursday 22 April to Friday 23 April 2004.

The objective of the ODRL International Workshop is to bring
together the research and industry communities to share
experiences and discuss the future developments of the language
to ensure its timeliness, usability, openness, and future success.

The Workshop Program is now available and includes Keynotes from
W3C, OMA, CoreMedia, T-Systems, Sun, and numerous invited speakers
and paper presentations.

The complete details, including Registration information, is
available from:

   <http://odrl.net/workshop2004/>


Renato Iannella & Susanne Guth
